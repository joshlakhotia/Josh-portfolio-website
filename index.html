About Me

Hi! I'm Josh, an American full stack developer and air sports enthusiast living in Salt Lake City, Utah. I'm always looking for the next big challenge or learning opportunity, whether that be in coding, skydiving, BASE jumping, or speedflying!

My web development skills are currently at the level of basic HTML, CSS, and vanilla Javascript and transitioning to the use of frameworks such as React. I have a basic understanding of Git and Github and will soon be learning backend development with node/express.js and PostgreSQL databases.

While my main focus at this point is web development, I am constantly exploring other technologies. Blockchain, especially solidity and rust, are next on my list of topics to pursue.
